CODE CRAFT-MASTERING FLASK: DAY 2

Teaching our Peers about Flask

Today, we took the opportunity to share our knowledge with our peers about the wonders of Flask. We covered the basics of building a web application using Flask, from setting up a new project to creating routes and templates.

We also delved into more advanced topics, such as using databases and creating APIs. Our peers were engaged and asked thoughtful questions, making it a great learning experience for everyone involved.

We explored the benefits of using Flask, including its lightweight and flexible nature, making it an ideal choice for building web applications. We also discussed the importance of security and best practices when building a Flask application.

The session was interactive, with hands-on exercises and live coding demonstrations. Our peers were able to get a feel for how Flask works and were able to ask questions and clarify any doubts they had.
