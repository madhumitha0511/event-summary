CODE CRAFT-MASTERING FLASK: DAY 1

Welcome to Day 1 of Code Craft!

The first day of Code Craft - Mastering Flask is all about assessing your basic knowledge of web development and programming concepts. This day is crucial in setting the tone for the rest of the event, and it's where we separate the participants who are ready to take their skills to the next level from those who need a bit more practice.

Objective

To assess participants' basic knowledge of web development and programming concepts, and to select participants for the next stages based on their performance.

Activities

  • Brief welcome and overview of the event's objectives and structure
  • Quiz: A structured quiz covering topics such as:
    • Basic Python programming
    • HTML/CSS fundamentals
    • Basic Flask framework concepts
    • General web development knowledge
  • Filtering: Based on quiz results, participants are selected to continue to the next stage

Filtering Criteria

Criteria for selection can include:

  • Score thresholds
  • Relative performance metrics

What to Expect

By the end of Day 1, you'll have a clear understanding of your strengths and weaknesses in web development and programming concepts.

If you're selected to continue to the next stage, you'll have the opportunity to learn from our expert instructors and work on real-world projects.
